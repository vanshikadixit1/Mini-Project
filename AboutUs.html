About IEEE GLAU

IEEE is the trusted voice for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.
The Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineering is commonly known as IEEE.
In a quest to promote women engineers and scientists, IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE)
functions as one of the largest global IEEE networks. It comprises IEEE member and volunteers.
Apart from the women scientists & engineers, the group also inspires budding girl engineers
to follow their academic interests and find their goals.

IEEE WIE aims to :-

  • Supports women's outstanding performance in computer science, electrical and electronic engineering.
  • Organize the major technical conferences for the enhancement of Networking.
  • Provide assistance to support ongoing activities by forming new WIE affinity groups
  • Encourage member grade advancement.
  • Favor the participation of women in leadership roles in IEEE governance.
